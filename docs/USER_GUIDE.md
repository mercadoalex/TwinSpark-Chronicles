# TwinSpark Chronicles — User Guide

A guide for parents and children using TwinSpark Chronicles.

## What Is TwinSpark Chronicles?

TwinSpark Chronicles is an interactive storytelling app where siblings create and experience personalized adventures together. An AI storyteller generates unique dual-perspective narratives — each child sees the story from their own point of view, making every session a shared but personal experience.

## Getting Started

### Step 1: Privacy Consent

When you first open the app, a privacy screen explains how data is used. This is required before anything else. Tap "Accept" to continue.

### Step 2: Choose a Language

Pick English or Spanish. The entire story experience adapts to your choice.

### Step 3: Create Your Characters

Each child fills in their profile:
- Name
- Gender
- Spirit animal (Dragon, Unicorn, Owl, Dolphin, Phoenix, or Tiger) — this shapes their character's personality and voice
- Toy companion name — a magical sidekick that appears in the story

You can also upload family photos. The app detects faces and can place family members into story scenes as art-style characters.

Once both profiles are set, confetti flies and the adventure begins.

### Step 4: Resume or Start Fresh

If you've played before, the app offers to continue your last adventure or start a new one. Your progress is automatically saved.

## The Story Experience

### How Stories Work

The AI generates a new story beat each turn. Each beat includes:
- A narrated scene (text + optional voice narration)
- A scene illustration (AI-generated in a Pixar/Disney style)
- Dual perspectives — the left panel shows one child's view, the right shows the other's
- Interactive choices at the bottom — tap one to steer the story

The story remembers what happened in previous beats and even previous sessions. Characters, locations, and items persist across adventures.

### Collaborative Drawing

Sometimes the AI invites the children to draw together. A drawing canvas appears with:
- Color palette, brushes, stamps, and eraser
- A countdown timer
- Sound effects and haptic feedback when selecting tools
- A celebration when the drawing is complete

The drawing gets woven into the next story beat.

### Scene Audio

Ambient sounds play to match the story setting — forest sounds, ocean waves, cave echoes. Tap "🔊 Tap to hear sounds" to enable audio on first visit.

### Voice Recordings

Parents or family members can pre-record voice messages (story intros, encouragements, sound effects) that play at relevant moments during the story.

## Parent Features

### Parent Controls (⚙️ icon)

- Content filters: set allowed themes and blocked words
- Time extensions: add +10, +15, or +30 minutes to the session
- Photo review: approve or reject photos flagged by the safety scanner
- Session history: see when the last session ended and why

### Parent Dashboard (gear icon, top-right)

Analytics and insights about your children's sessions — personality profiles, sibling dynamics, session summaries.

### Sibling Dashboard (collapsible panel, top-right)

Real-time view of sibling dynamics during a session — who's leading, relationship score, skill discoveries, turn tracking.

## Session Timer

A countdown timer runs during each session. The default is 30 minutes (configurable in Parent Controls).

- The timer pauses automatically while the AI is generating content
- Parents can extend time from Parent Controls
- Under 10 seconds, the timer pulses red as a visual warning
- When time runs out, a gentle Wind Down Screen appears with a goodbye message and star-trail animation before returning to the home screen

## Emergency Stop

If you need to end the session immediately, press and hold the stop button for 2 seconds. A progress ring fills during the hold. On release before 2 seconds, nothing happens (prevents accidental stops). After 2 seconds, the session saves and the Wind Down Screen plays a shorter 4-second goodbye.

## Story Gallery (📚 icon)

Completed stories are automatically archived. Open the gallery to browse past adventures and read them back as storybooks, page by page.

## World Map (🗺️ icon)

A persistent map of all locations, NPCs, and items discovered across sessions. The story world grows with each adventure.

## Saving and Exiting

- Progress auto-saves after every story beat
- If you close the browser tab, a snapshot is saved via the Beacon API
- "Save & Exit" archives the story to the gallery
- "Exit Without Saving" discards the current session

## Tips

- Let each child take turns making choices — the app tracks whose turn it is
- Try different spirit animals for different story flavors
- Upload family photos to see family members appear as characters in scenes
- Record voice messages in the Voice Library for a personal touch
- The story world persists — revisit locations and meet NPCs from previous adventures
